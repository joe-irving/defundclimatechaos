October

5 Oct

Framing Climate Justice

1:00 PM to 2:00 PM

Find out the Framing Climate Project's learning about why values-based communications are effective at winning hearts and minds, how to effectively communicate about climate crisis to the general public and how to embed justice-focused messaging into your actions and campaigns.

6 Oct

Defund Fossil Fuels: Banks Activism

12:00 PM to 1:00 PM

An introduction to UK banks’ role in funding climate chaos. We'll explore which banks are funding the fossil fuel industry and the projects they are financing. Participants of this session will also hear from people at the frontline of resistance against projects financed by UK banks, understanding the nature of their struggle

7 Oct

Group Care and Regenerative Activism

5:30 PM to 6:30 PM

This workshop aims to introduce ideas about what a caring, supportive and regenerative culture could look like in your groups. In the session, we’ll try out tools that can help to nurture a slow paced and anti-oppressive culture that can prevent burnout while being attentive to power dynamics in your groups.

9 Oct

Absorbing Momentum

2:00 PM to 4:00 PM

With COP26 just around the corner, this workshop explores the concepts of momentum-driven organising and will share practical tactics for absorbing new people and energy into your group and campaigns around key mobilisations like October 29th and whirlwind moments like COP26.

10 Oct

Art and Action

11:00 AM to 1:00 PM

Learn more about a range of creative direct action tactics used around the world. This workshop will explore how to use art, memorial and powerful stories to engage more people and further your campaign goals. Followed by breakout spaces for detailed action planning in smaller regional groups to make plans for finance and divestment days of action ahead of COP26.
